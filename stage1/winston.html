Winston

Biography

  • Name: Winston
  • Age: 29
  • Occupation: Scientist, Adventurer
  • Base of Operations: Horizon Lunar Colony (formerly)
  • Affiliation: Overwatch (formerly)

STORY

To create a big step in humanity's space exploration, the Horizon Lunar Colony was created. Some of the residents were genetically enhanced gorillas that were intended to test the effects of prolonged life in space. One ape in particular displayed rapid brain development and was taken under the wing of Dr. Harold Winston, who educated the ape in science and told him fascinating tales of human discovery.

As the young ape grew older, he experienced an uprising from the other gorillas that killed the scientists and claimed the colony for their own. He decided to take on the name of his beloved human caretaker, Winston, and built a makeshift rocket that he used to travel to Earth. Scared and unsure what to do, he was luckily picked up by Overwatch and found a new home. There he saw light in humanity and was able to conduct his own research and become a hero like he always admired in Harold.

After Overwatch ended, Winston had disappeared to a lab in Gibraltar, cut off from humanity and once again feeling isolated, longing over the days for heroes to return.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Tank

Winston's arsenal is very versatile and allows him to be a strong asset to any team fight. His suit has a Jump Pack function which allows him to leap into enemy back lines and eleminate smaller targets using his Tesla Cannon. He can also deploy a Barrier Projector to shield allies and block enemy attacks.

ABILITIES

Tesla Cannon

A short-range electric barrage that locks onto enemy targets and can inflict damage upon several opponents at once.

Jump Pack

Allows a large leap into the air to deal significant damage to enemies when landing and can be used for positioning in strategic locations.

Barrier Projector

Deploys a large bubble-shaped shield that absorbs damage taken to protect enemies inside or on the opposing side of enemies. Allies can fire through it to attack enemies.

Primal Rage

Induces an animalistic nature that increases health and melee damage for a brief period of time. The Jump Pack ability can also be used in quicker succession while active.
