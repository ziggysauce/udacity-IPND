Hanzo

Biography

  • Name: Hanzo Shimada
  • Age: 38
  • Occupation: Mercenary, Assassin
  • Base of Operations: Hanamura, Japan (formerly)
  • Affiliation: Shimada Clan

STORY

Centuries ago the Shimada clan established themselves as assassins with growing power and eventually the creation of a criminal empire that profitted from lucrative trade in arms and illegal substances. Hanzo, the eldest son of the clan leader, was bound by duty to succeed his father and rule the Shimada empire. He was trained intensively in martial arts, swordplay, and bowmanship, all while being prepared for immense responsibilties, leadership and knowledge of strategy and tactics.

Following the death of the clan's leader, Hanzo was left with the task to ensure his younger brother Genji was on the correct path towards success in helping rule the Shuamada empire. His brother however, was young and reckless, and did not want to take any responsibilities. This lead to family conflict which ultimately put Hanzo in a position to kill his own brother. This crippled Hanzo with guilt and drove him mad, rejecting his father's legacy and abandoning the clan altogether.

Now Hanzo travels the world, mastering his skills as a warrior, attempting to regain his honor and put the past behind him.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Defense

Hanzo's arsenal is very versatile in its arrow abilities. He can reveal his enemies or send multiple arrows at once with different specialized arrows. He has the ability to scale walls in order to gain a vantage point over his enemies and can summon a titanic spirit dragon to slay all opponents in its path.

ABILITIES

Storm Bow

A powerful bow that fires various arrows at enemy targets.

Sonic Arrow

Can be accessed to send an arrow revealing nearby enemies using sonar tracking.

Scatter Arrow

Can be accessed to send fragmenting arrows that ricochet off walls and objects to strike multiple targets at once.

Dragonstrike

Summons a Spirit Dragon that detroys all enemies in its path; can travel through walls.
