McCree

Biography

  • Name: Jesse McCree
  • Age: 37
  • Occupation: Bounty Hunter
  • Base of Operations: Santa Fe, New Mexico, USA
  • Affiliation: Overwatch (formerly)

STORY

As a member of the Deadlock Gang, Jesse McCree smuggled illegal weapons and military hardware through the American Soutwest for years until his group was busted by an Overwatch operation. Due to his excellent skillset of a sharpshooter and resourcefulness, he was given the opportunity to rot in prison or use his potential and join the covert Blackwatch division of Overwatch.

Though cynical at first, McCree chose to join Blackwatch in hopes of erasing his past sins of an outlaw. In time, conflict rose within Overwatch as they lost power, leading McCree not wanting anything to do with the infighting. He turned to going underground and becoming a shadow.

After several years McCree resurfaced as a mercenary for hire, becoming one of the most talented bounty hunters abailable. However, while he is seen as a great commodity to possess on your team, he only accepts contracts of which he believes are for a just cause.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Attack

McCree's arsenal is incredible simplistic but powerful. He has a Peacekeeper pistol that shoots six rounds of incredible blast power and can be fanned to give a quick burst attack. He has the ability to Combat Roll to avoid enemies and automatically reload his rounds. His kit is eqipped with a Flashbang as well to stun enemies in their tracks for defeat. Lastly, McCree can summon his Deadeye which pinpoints all targets in range to deliver a devasting blow from his pistol.

ABILITIES

Peacekeeper

A six-bullet pistol with tremendous power that can spray a fan of the entire round in one bout if necessary.

Combat Roll

A swift dive in any direction to either avoid enemy attacks or to gain better poisitioning to attack opponents. While doing so, McCree's pistol is automatically reloaded.

Flashbang

A blinding flashbang grenade can be thrown at or near enemies to temporarily stun them allowing McCree and his allies to punish them.

Deadeye

Momentarily focuses with pinpoint accuracy to target several enemies at once with the Peacekeeper. As enemies get weaker, aiming gets faster and the damage potential icnreases. McCree will be vulnerable however, with slow movement speed and a red glow to his body.
