Tracer

Biography

  • Name: Lena Oxton
  • Age: 26
  • Occupation: Adventurer
  • Base of Operations: London, England
  • Affiliation: Overwatch (formerly)

STORY

Lena Oxton was the youngest recruit Overwatch brought in for their experimental flight program due to her fearless piloting skills. She was handpicked to test the prototype of a teleporting fighter, the Slipstream. Unfortunately, during the test the teleportation matrix malfunctioned and Lena had disappeared, presumably dead.

Months passed, but eventually Lena had returned with her molecules desynchronized from the flow of time. She became a living ghost and would disappear for hours or days at a time, struggling to maintain a physical form. All hope for Lena was lost until a scientist named Winston designed a chronal accelerator that allowed her to stay anchored to present time. Additionally, it gave her the ability to control her own time and zoom forward in the blink of an eye, or rewind time. This made her a valuable asset to Overwatch and she donned the name of Tracer.

Following the fall of Overwatch, Tracer remains in the fight for good and helps wherever needed.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Attack

Tracer's arsenal is designed to be quick and effective. Her kit includes powerful Pulse Pistols to quickly melt enemies. She has the abilitiy to blink to new locations and can Recall back to avoid damage and restore health.

ABILITIES

Pulse Pistols

A rapid-fire dual-wielded pistol to shred enemies quickly.

Blink

The ability to instantly zip through space in any direction.

Recall

The ability to rewind backwards a few seconds in time to avoid enemy attacks, recharge ammunition, and restore health.

Pulse Bomb

Can throw out a large bomb that adheres to surfaces and explodes a large damage output to all enemies within the blast radius.
