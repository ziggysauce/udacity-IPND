STORY

Originally the Commander of Overwatch, Jack Morrison suffered having to watch Overwatch be destroyed from within. While not confirmed, it's rumored that he donned the name of Soldier: 76 and went through the American "soldier enhancement program". He became a vigilante, found to strike financial institutions, corrupt corporations, and Overwatch facilities. While his actions are seen as inscrutable, many claim to believe that he's actually shedding light on the truth behind the collapse of Overwatch.

With an unrelenting search to discover the truth and to bring justice to those responsible for Overwatch's demise, Soldier: 76 continues the hunt and fights for good. He has become a target of an international manhunt, but for good reason.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Attack

Soldier: 76's arsenal is advanced and extremely versatile. His kit is equipped with cutting-edge technology, arming him with a Heavy Pulse Rifle that fires fully-automatic rounds towards enemies and can release Helix Rockets to deliver a devastating blow. He has super human sprinting speed and can setup a biotic field to heal himself and surrounding allies.

ABILITIES

Heavy Pulse Rifle

An advanced weapon that fires a high damage output.

Helix Rockets

A triple array of mini rockets that gets shot out of the Pulse Rifle in a single burst towards opponents. The rockets deliver explosive damage to enemies within a small radius.

Sprint

The ability to sprint with super human speed in order to evade an attack or to rush ahead to gain an edge over the enemy.

Biotic Field

Can setup a field of regeneration to heal all allies within a small radius for a short period of time.

Tactical Visor

Can equip a vision assist that pinpoints enemy targets and locks them into view for laser accuracy firing. If an enemy leaves the line of sight, a new one can quickly be targeted.
