Widowmaker

Biography

  • Name: Amélie Lacroix
  • Age: 33
  • Occupation: Assassin
  • Base of Operations: Annecy, France
  • Affiliation: Talon

STORY

Amélie was the wife of Gérard Lacroix, an Overwatch agent that lead the operations in taking down the terrorist group Talon. Over several attempts to eleminate him, Talon instead took an approach of abducting his wife Amélie. They were able to successfully reprogram her through neural reconditioning which broke her will, suppressed her personality, and eventually turned her into a sleeper agent. She was eventually discovered by Overwatch agents, but two weeks later, killed her husband in his sleep.

Amélie returned to Talon and finished her transition into a living weapon. She was trained extensively for battle and even went through physiological changes that slowed her heart, turning her cold and blue, and made her an excellent sniper and a ruthless killer with no emotion.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Defense

Widowmaker's arsenal is equpped to quickly destroy small targets and heavily weaken larger tagets from a great distance.

ABILITIES

Widow's Kiss

A versatile sniper rifle that deals a heavy amount of damage when fired in scope view. At closer ranges, it can be used as a fully-automatic gun for defense.

Grappling Hook

Can aim the hook to grapple towards better positioning for an advantage over enemies or to evade a fight.

Venom Mine

Can shoot out an adhesive mine that triggers when an opponent is nearby, releasing a poisonous gas.

Infra-Sight

Provides visual of all enemy target locations through walls and objects for a brief period of time for all allies to see.
