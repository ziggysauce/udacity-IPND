Lúcio

Biography

  • Name: Lúcio Correia dos Santos
  • Age: 26
  • Occupation: DJ, Freedom Fighter
  • Base of Operations: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
  • Affiliation: None

STORY

Raised in the poor favelas of Rio de Janeiro, Lúcio suffered his hometown being hit by a financial unheaval following the Omnic Crisis. As Brazil recovered, he sought a way to lift spirits around those who were having trouble with the aftermath. The power of music became Lúcio's main asset as he quickly became a a pretty-well known legend among the streets of Brazil.

Devastation yet again struck when the multinational Vishkar Corporation solidifed a deal to redevelop large tracts of the city. The people were promised improvement in their lives, but those promises were left unfulfilled. Vishkar instead became an order that controlled the areas with curfews, lawless behavior, and exploitation of cheap labor force.

Upset by these results, Lúcio stole Vishkar's sonic technology that was originally used to suppress the people, and instead used it to rally them into action. His leadership brought upon the removal of Vishkar and landed him the icon of an international symbol of positive social change. His music became the sound of hope and he became a global celebrity. Lúcio has since strived to make a difference in the world through his music and uplifting social change.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Support

Lúcio's arsenal is equppied with Sonic Amplifiers that strike enemies with projectiles and can bump them away with sound waves. His songs can both heal allies or provide a speed boost if nearby.

ABILITIES

Sonic Amplifier

Strikes enemies with sonic projectiles or can knock them back.

Crossfade

The ability to switch between two songs that continuously either heal allies or provides a speed boost.

Amp It Up

An increase in the volume boosts the effects of whichever song is being played.

Sound Barrier

An outburst of waves resonates from the Sonic Amplifier providing a brief spike in surrounding allies' shields for protection.
