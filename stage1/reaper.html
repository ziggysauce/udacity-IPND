STORY

Known as a figure in a black robe, Reaper is a terrorist known to cause havoc with intentions unknown. What is know, is that death follows wherever he goes. For decades he has been found fighting in various conflicts showing zero loyalty to any group or organization. Survivors tell tales of a shadowed figure floating through the battlefield, untouched by any attacks.

Rumors of a speculated genetic alteration failure have come and gone, leaving everyone unprepared for what powers Reaper holds. There is one thing that they have noticed though, he appears to follow a pattern: hunting former Overwach agents. There is speculation that the true identity of Reaper is Gabriel Reyes, a former Overwatch agent and Blackwatch coordinator, with thoughts that he may be behind the fall of Overwatch.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Attack

Reaper's arsenal is equipped with mega powerful twin shotguns that deal incredible amounts of damage. He can enter wraith form to avoid taking damage and can also teleport to vantage points giving him an edge over his enemies.

ABILITIES

Hellfire Shotguns

A powerful set of twin shotguns that deals a ton of damage to enemy foes.

Wraith Form

Transformation into a shadow allowing Reaper to avoid taking enemy damage and can travel to safety unharmed. Reaper cannot attack while this is in effect.

Shadow Step

An ability to teleport to a different location to gain a better vantage point for battle.

Death Blossom

Emptying of both twin shotguns at rapid speed dealing heavy amounts of damage to all enemies within Reaper's radius.
