Biography

  • Name: ░░░░░░ (known as Sombra)
  • Age: 30
  • Occupation: Hacker
  • Base of Operations: Dorado, Mexico
  • Affiliation: Talon, Los Muertos (formerly)

STORY

At a young age, "Sombra" was one of many children who was left orphaned due to the Omnic Crisis. The country's infrastructure had been destroyed and she only survived by tapping into her potential to hack computers. Later in age, she was inducted into Los Muertos, a gang believed to be rebuilding Mexico against the government's ways that benefited the rich and left the poor with very little help.

A skilled hacker with mastery in evasion and detection, Sombra eventually found herself tangled in a global conspiracy that compromised her security. She was forced to delete any trace of her existence and went into hiding.

When she finally re-emerged, she donned the title of Sombra and was determined to discover the truth behind the conspiracy that she had once uncovered. She began a string of hacks across the globe which eventually brought attention to Talon who recruited her. She joined, believing that her contributions would be against corporations with strong affiliations to the government. She was right, and eventually her efforts of a massive cyberattack caused a revolt in Mexico against LumériCo and a security breach of Volskaya Industries.

Now that Sombra is a part of Talon and has their trust, she can focus on her own personal goal: unraveling the truth behind the conspiracy and instead using its power for her own means.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Attack

Sombra's arsenal is equpped to quickly tear down an enemy's defense and shred through weaker targets unexpected. She wields a Machine Pistol that fires a short-range spread, and can hack opponents causing temporary holds on their abilities. Her kit also allows her to evade enemies and sneakily get into the backline with her Stealth and Translocator abilities.

ABILITIES

Machine Pistol

A fully-automatic weapon that fires a spread of short-range bullets to inflict quick and precise damage to nearby targets.

Hack

Taps into an enemy's kit and temporarily stops them from using their abilities. Also able to hack healthpacks rendering them useless to opponents.

Stealth

Can temporarily become invisible and gain a speed boost to get behind the enemy lines and strike them where they least expect. When attacking or taking any damage, the stealth ability is disabled.

Translocator

Can setup a beacon and instantly teleport to it when needed if it's active.

EMP

A massive discharge that cyberhacks all nearby opponents in wide radius, destroying enemy barries and shields.
