Zarya

Biography

  • Name: Aleksandra Zaryanova
  • Age: 28
  • Occupation: Soldier
  • Base of Operations: Krasnoyarsk Front, Russia
  • Affiliation: Russian Defense Forces

STORY

Aleksandra was born in a remote Siberian village that was attacked during the Omnic Crisis. While the Russian forces were able to destroy the robots, the surrounding lands were also devasted in the aftermath of the battle. Aleksandra, a child during these events, was surrounded by destruction and war. She grew to be the world's strongest woman and vowed to help her people recover.

Recognized for her extensive training and abilities, Aleksandra climbed the ranks of the national athletics program and represented her country in the upcoming world championships. The night before she was able to compete however, an attack came down on her village and had once again been victim to war. She immediately withdrew from the competition and returned home to defend her people.

Though she gave up a life of fame and stardom, she is seen more as a heroic symbol and a beacon of hope to those that believe she can help restore their contry from the loss of their loved ones.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Tank

Zarya's arsenal is extremely powerful if used correctly. She wields a Particle Cannon that melts enemies and can gain damage output if either herself or her allies takes any damage while in a barrier that she projects.

ABILITIES

Particle Cannon

Can unleash a steady beam of damage to close-ranged targets or project an explosive charge to strike several enemies at a distance.

Particle Barrier

The Particle Cannon can create a personal barrier to surround Zarya and shield her from incoming attacks. Any damage taken redirects the energy to power up the width and damage of its beam when attacking.

Projected Barrier

The Particle Cannon can create a personal barrier to surround an ally and shield them from incoming attacks. Any damage taken redirects the energy to power up the Particle Cannon when attacking.

Graviton Surge

Can launch a gravity bomb that draws in enemies and deals damage while trapped. Incredibly powerful for crowd control and to end a team fight.
