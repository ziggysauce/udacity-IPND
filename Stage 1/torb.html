Torbjörn

Biography

  • Name: Torbjörn Lindholm
  • Age: 57
  • Occupation: Weapons Designer
  • Base of Operations: Gothenburg, Sweden
  • Affiliation: Overwatch (formerly)

STORY

A brilliant engineer, Torbjörn was recognized for designing weapons systems used by countries all over the world before the Omnic Crisis. Employers began to believe that weapons should be using networked computer intelligence which he had a quarrel with. He was paranoid of mistrust in sentient robotic intelligence, and his fears became a reality when the robot populations eventually turned on their human creators during the Omnic Crisis.

He was quickly recruited by Overwatch for his skills and joined the original strike team to fight off the uprising. With his help, Overwatch eventually became known as the international peacekeeping force, and he remained loyal to the organization as the head engineer for technological needs and signature weapons systems.

Unfortunately, following the fall of Overwatch, many of Torbjörn's weapons were stolen or hidden. Torbjörn felt responsible for the creation of powerful weapons that may now be in the hands of corruption and evil. He remains determined to stop them from wreaking havoc upon the innocent.

HERO OVERVIEW

Role: Defense

Torbjörn's arsenal is mechanically sound in building a high powered turret to assist allies in a team fight. His kit is equipped with a Rivet Gun to blast enemies down, and also a Forge Hammer to upgrade his turrets to maximize damage and speed. In battle, he can collect scrap and create armor packs for allies.

ABILITIES

Rivet Gun

Can fire long-range projectiles and eject close-range bursts of molten metal to damage enemies.

Forge Hammer

Can be used to build, upgrade, and repair turrets. It can also be swung as a weapon to deal damage to foes.

Build Turret

Can construct an enemy-tracking autocannon that can be repaired or upgraded using the Forge Hammer.

Armor Pack

Can throw out an armor upgrade for allies on the battlefield or for himself.

Molten Core

Temporarily overheats all weapons systems and gives Torbjörn increased armor and damage output. His turrets are also upgraded and spit out faster cannons to deal more damage.
